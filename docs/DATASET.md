# Dataset (Synthetic) - Directed Speech (RU)

The dataset is generated by `scripts/generate_data_v3.py`.

## Labels
- `label=1` - utterance is directed to the assistant (command/question/dialog control)
- `label=0` - utterance is not directed to the assistant (human conversation, background, small talk)

## Columns
- `text` - text (often after ASR-like corruption)
- `label` - 0/1
- `group_id` - template group identifier used to prevent leakage during split

## Noise model (ASR approximation)
Probabilistic transformations are applied:
- filler insertion ("em", "uh", ...)
- punctuation/comma removal
- random character errors (deletion/substitution)
- partial phrases (truncation at beginning/end)
- random lowercasing

## Post-noise validation for positives
After noise injection, positive examples are validated so that empty/degenerate commands made only of fillers are filtered out.

## Known limitations
- Synthetic data does not cover the full range of spoken language and dialects.
- Real ASR quality depends on domain and may require threshold tuning (`threshold`).
